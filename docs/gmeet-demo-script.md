# DealWhisper Live Demo — Google Meet Script

> **Duration:** 4 minutes | **People:** 2 (Seller + Buyer) | **Platform:** Google Meet + DealWhisper

---

## Roles

| Person | Role | What they need |
|--------|------|----------------|
| **You** (Seller) | Sales rep selling enterprise SaaS ($420K/year) | Chrome with DealWhisper + Google Meet |
| **Friend** (Buyer) | Sarah Chen, VP of Operations at Acme Corp | Google Meet only — nothing to install |

---

## Before the Call

**Send your friend this message:**

> Hey! I need your help with a 3-minute demo. You're playing a buyer named Sarah Chen, VP of Operations at Acme Corp. You're evaluating our software vs Salesforce. Your CFO wants ROI proof. You think pricing is steep but you're interested. I'll send you 4 lines to say during the call — just say them naturally and push back when I respond. That's it!

---

## The Script

### ACT 1 — Pre-Call Briefing (1 min, solo)

Open DealWhisper. Fill in buyer context:

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Buyer name | Sarah Chen |
| Company | Acme Corp |
| Title | VP of Operations |
| Goal | Close the renewal and expand into Q3 implementation |
| Anchor | $420K / year |
| Floor | $340K / year |
| Watch for | Budget objection, Salesforce comparison, timeline delays |
| Your edge | Only vendor with HIPAA-certified real-time pipeline |

Click **Start briefing**. Tap the **mic button** and say naturally:

> "Sarah mentioned last quarter she's comparing us against Salesforce. Her boss, the CFO, wants ROI numbers before signing anything. She pushed back on the timeline last time we spoke but seemed genuinely interested in the onboarding package."

Answer 1–2 AI follow-up questions by voice or text. Then click **Go to live session**.

---

### ACT 2 — Go Live (30 sec)

1. Click **Start session (mic + screen)**
2. Browser asks for mic permission → Allow
3. Browser asks which screen to share → Pick the **Google Meet tab**
4. Join Google Meet where your friend is waiting
5. DealWhisper is now listening and watching

---

### ACT 3 — The Negotiation (2 min)

> **Instructions for your friend:** Say these 4 lines naturally throughout the conversation. Paraphrase them — don't read word-for-word. Add "um", "honestly", "look" to sound real. Push back when the seller responds.

---

**LINE 1 — Opening objection**

**FRIEND (Buyer):**
> "Hey, thanks for jumping on. So we've been going through the numbers internally and honestly, the pricing feels steep compared to what Salesforce quoted us last week."

**What DealWhisper should show:** Objection pattern detected (price resistance) + Competitor mention (Salesforce) + Deal temperature shift

**YOU:** Respond naturally. Read the coaching whisper and use it.

---

**LINE 2 — Authority + timeline pressure**

**FRIEND (Buyer):**
> "Our CFO wants to see clear ROI within six months before she'll approve anything. Can you actually guarantee that kind of timeline?"

**What DealWhisper should show:** Authority signal (CFO decision-maker) + Timeline pressure + BANT update (Authority confirmed)

**YOU:** Respond using the whisper guidance.

---

**LINE 3 — The negotiation moment**

**FRIEND (Buyer):**
> "Look, if you can get it under three-eighty and throw in the onboarding package, I think I can get this signed by end of month."

**What DealWhisper should show:** Close window detected + Deal temperature rising + Buying signal (commitment language)

**YOU:** This is the close. The whisper should guide you on whether to accept or counter.

---

**LINE 4 — Commitment**

**FRIEND (Buyer):**
> "Alright, that works. Send me the revised proposal and I'll take it to the leadership team on Thursday."

**What DealWhisper should show:** Commitment signal + Next steps detected + Stage transition to "Closing"

**YOU:** Confirm next steps, thank them, end the meeting.

---

### ACT 4 — Post-Call Debrief (30 sec)

1. Click **End session** in DealWhisper
2. Switch to the **Post-Call Debrief** tab
3. Show what the AI generated:
   - Follow-up email draft (ready to send)
   - Deal score and performance metrics
   - Coaching feedback: what worked / what to improve
   - CRM-ready summary

**Say to camera:** "That's DealWhisper — real-time AI coaching that listens to your sales calls, reads the room, and whispers strategy while you sell."

---

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Screen share permission denied | Click "Start session" again — must allow on first prompt |
| No whispers appearing | Make sure your friend is speaking loud enough for your mic to pick up |
| Whispers are slow | Normal — AI processes in 2-3 second cycles |
| Friend's audio not captured | Share the Google Meet **tab** (not window), enable "Share tab audio" |

---

## Tips

- **Friend should paraphrase**, not read verbatim — sounds way more natural
- Leave 10-15 seconds between friend's lines for whispers to appear
- If a whisper gives great advice, say "Actually that's a great point..." and use it — shows the product working
- Keep Google Meet and DealWhisper side by side so the recording captures both
- Record the Google Meet for the demo video submission
